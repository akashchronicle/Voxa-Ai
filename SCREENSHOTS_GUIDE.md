#### **Image Format & Size**
- **Format**: PNG or JPG
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 or higher (for crisp display)

- **Consistent Style**: Use same browser/device for all screenshots
- **Professional Look**: Remove browser bookmarks, extensions, etc.
